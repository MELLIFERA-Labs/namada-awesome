## *Summary*

The Validator Community call #3 covered updates for the most recent testnet, including more distributed voting power, implementation of Gas and Fees, and the need to fund accounts and use gas and fees flags to prepare for the next testnet. A guide to all the transaction types to test was provided, and the grPGF function will be tested in the next testnet.
